Built for togetherness

We're built for real life—friends, groups, and the goals you care about. Every feature is designed so you stay accountable together.

From shared goals to bets and dares, we keep it simple: plan with your crew, track progress in one place, and level up with the same people who show up day one.

Dares

When someone misses a goal, the group decides the consequence. Dares keep everyone in the game and turn slip-ups into shared moments—together.

Bets

Put real stakes on the line with friends. Bets turn goals into shared wins and keep everyone accountable—so leveling up feels worth it, together.

Togetherness

About the creator

Meet the young developer behind Bttr Together

Moustapha Gueye

Founder & Developer

As-salāmu 'alaykum wa rahmatullah,

My name is Moustapha, and I am the creator of Bttr Together. I am 18 years old at the time of release, and I have been Muslim my entire life, alhamdulillah. Although I was born into Islam, I understand that many people come to Islam later in life, and I have immense respect for those who find their way to the truth.

This app was developed entirely by me. Every part of it — the design, the code, the content — was created with care, intention, and the hope that it could make a difference in someone's life.

Bttr Together is not just an app; it's a project of the heart. It's my way of giving back and using the skills that Allah has blessed me with to benefit others, in shā' Allah. I am still learning and growing, and I hope this app reflects that journey.

May Allah guide us all, increase us in knowledge, and make this effort a source of reward.

Thank you for being here. I truly wish you the best in your journey.

Was-salāmu 'alaykum wa rahmatullāhi wa barakatuh.

— Moustapha Gueye, Founder
